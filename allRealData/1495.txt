Senator Charles Schumer (D-NY) talked about what went wrong for the Democratic Party in the 2014 midterm elections, and its plans for the 2016 elections. He talked about his plans for a new “pact with the middle class,” saying that Democrats could become the dominant party for a generation if they showed the middle class that government could work for them.
