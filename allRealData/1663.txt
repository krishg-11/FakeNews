President Trump in Moon Township, Pennsylvania

March 10, 2018

2018-03-10T22:31:03-05:00

https://images.c-span.org/Files/da4/20180310223751001_hd.jpg

President Trump campaigned in the Pittsburgh area for Rick Saccone, the Republican candidate in the March 13, 2018, 18th Congressional District special election. The president talked about a wide range of issues including his new steel and aluminum tariffs, the plan to meet with North Korean leader Kim Jong-un, the Republican tax reform law, and tougher penalties for drug dealers.
