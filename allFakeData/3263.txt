See Kathie Lee Gifford debut her new song ‘He Saw Jesus’ on TODAY

Inspired by her late husband, Frank Gifford, Kathie Lee Gifford teamed up with country songwriter Brett James to write an emotional new song. In her first musical performance on TODAY since 2008, Kathie Lee performs “He Saw Jesus.”
