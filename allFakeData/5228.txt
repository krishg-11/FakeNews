Here Is the Complete List of Winners From the 2017 Billboard Music Awards

Drake was the big winner at the 2017 Billboard Music Awards.

In fact, the rapper took home 13 awards, making him the artist with the most in a single year -- beating Adele's record of 12.

Billboard Music Awards nominees are based on key fan interactions with music, including album and digital songs sales, streaming, radio airplay, touring and social engagement. These measurements are tracked year-round by Billboard and its data partners, including Nielsen Music and Next Big Sound. The awards are based on the reporting period of March 18, 2016 through March 16, 2017.

The 2017 Billboard Music Awards pre-show begins at 6pm ET from the T-Mobile Magenta Carpet. The ceremony airs live on ABC at 8pm ET from the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas.
