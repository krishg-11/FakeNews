Christine Taylor on Wikinow

Christine Joan Taylor-Stiller is an American actress, best known for her roles as Melody Hanson on Hey Dude, Marcia Brady in The Brady Bunch Movie, Holly Sullivan in The Wedding Singer, Katherine Veatch in Dodgeball: A True Underdog Story, and Matilda Jeffries in Zoolander and Zoolander 2.
