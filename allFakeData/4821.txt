Jerry O'Connell

Jerry O'Connell was born in New York City, to Linda (Witkowski), an art teacher, and Michael O'Connell, a British-born advertising agency art director. He spent his early years in Manhattan, with his parents and younger brother, Charlie O'Connell , who is also an actor. He is of one half Irish, one quarter Italian, and one quarter Polish, descent. ...
