JAY-Z – Lucifer

About “Lucifer”

“Lucifer” is the 12th song off of Jay-Z’s The Black Album, produced by Jay-Z’s friend and frequent collaborator, Kanye West, who also wrote the line in the hook:

(I’m from the murder capital, where we murder for capital)

This song heavily relies​ on religious and Biblical references, which can be alluded to from the title.
