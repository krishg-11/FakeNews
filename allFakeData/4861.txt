Sandra Bullock, who plays the principal character Margaret Tate, is 12 years older than Ryan Reynolds, who plays Andrew Paxton, her husband-to-be. Bullock was born in 1964; Reynolds in 1976. In the movie script, Tate is 37 and Paxton is 26. The age difference is briefly referenced in the movie, but it's not a major part of the plot. Edit
