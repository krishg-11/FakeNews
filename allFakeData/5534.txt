Jennifer Hudson Teases R.Kelly-Assisted 'It's Your World': Listen

Jennifer Hudson performs onstage during the BET AWARDS '14 at Nokia Theatre L.A. LIVE on June 29, 2014 in Los Angeles, California.

After debuting "It's Your World" at the 2014 BET Awards on Sunday (June 29), Jennifer Hudson releases an official snippet of the single and other songs off her upcoming album.

The full version of her R.Kelly-assisted single leaked the day after her performance.

Before performing "It's Your World" on the main stage, J.Hud strutted down the aisles of the Nokia Theatre performing "Walk It Out," sans featured guest, Timbaland.

The album sampler features snippets of "It's Your World," "He Ain't Goin' Nowhere," "Dangerous," and full versions of "I Can't Describe (The Way I Feel)(featuring T.I.) and "Walk It Out."
