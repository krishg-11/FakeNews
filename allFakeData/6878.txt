Yom Kippur: Harry Styles & More Tweet On Jewish Day Of Atonement

Yom Kippur began tonight and ends tomorrow, Oct. 4. Stars like Harry Styles and Mayim Bialik have taken to Twitter to express their thoughts on the Jewish Day of Atonement.

Yom Kippur is one of the holiest holidays of the Jewish year. Jewish people around the world are pausing to fast, pray and attend services during this holy holiday. Hollywood’s hottest stars are wishing others a peaceful and meaningful Yom Kippur!
